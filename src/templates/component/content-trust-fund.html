Introduction to the USG Asbestos Trust

The United States Gypsum Asbestos Personal Injury Settlement Trust, formed under Chapter 11 of the US Bankruptcy Code, is designed to resolve all asbestos-related claims against the United States Gypsum Company (USG) for which the company is legally responsible.

Trust Purpose and Functions

The Trust primarily aims to compensate all valid asbestos personal injury claims against USG and certain associated parties. Compensations are distributed based on the Trust Distribution Procedures (TDP) approved by the bankruptcy court. This platform aims to provide comprehensive information to assist claimants and others in:

  • Understanding the purpose of the USG Asbestos Trust
  • Gaining insights into how the Trust addresses claims

Claim Submission and Resolution

The USG Asbestos Trust commenced the acceptance of claims relating to asbestos personal injury settlements on February 19, 2007, starting at 10:00 A.M. EST.

Current Payment Percentage

At present, the payment percentage for USG stands at 12.7%. This applies to both:

  • 12.7% of the scheduled value for Expedited Review
  • 12.7% of the gross settlement value for Individual Review
