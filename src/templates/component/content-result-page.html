Expedite Review

  • The person is categorized along with similar claims (pertaining to the same "level of illness" or type of disease) and given a preset reimbursement.

  • Basic data, including evidence of exposure and bills, are gathered.

  • The severity of the disease or degree of exposure is not considered, although this may expedite the reimbursement process.

Expedite Review

  • Each claim is evaluated on an individual basis for the claimant.

  • Several aspects are taken into account, including the quantity of dependents.

  • The compensation received may be greater or lesser than that obtained through a expediate review process.

AVG Claims/Settlementsw

  • In a mesothelioma agreement, the asbestos producer consents to pay a negotiated sum to settle an asbestos claim. This settlement amount is decided through discussions between your lawyer and the company that caused the asbestos exposure.

  • Settlements provide faster financial relief to families than verdicts by a jury or court. They are legally enforceable, and if the settlement is not paid as scheduled, legal measures can be enacted. Therefore, settlements ensure a guaranteed payout.
