Mesothelioma FAQs

Will Mesothelioma ever be curable?

Future advances in medical research could lead to a cure for Mesothelioma. Scientists and doctors are continuously researching and testing new treatments. Currently, there isn't a definitive cure, but treatments can help manage the disease and improve patients' quality of life. We get one step closer to finding a cure with each new discovery.
