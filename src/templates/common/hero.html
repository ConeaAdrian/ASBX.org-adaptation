Veterans and Mesothelioma

Many of our country's veterans, who showed extraordinary courage while serving, struggle with a hidden and ongoing enemy even after their active service. Asbestos exposure during their military service has led to severe illnesses, such as mesothelioma and lung cancer, which can take decades to develop.

Advocacy for Responsible Asbestos Management

One of our core objectives at ASBX.org is to champion policy change that strengthens regulations and promotes responsible asbestos management. We work tirelessly to advocate for stricter legislation and increased enforcement to protect the public from the dangers of asbestos exposure. Join us in our fight for a safer future by supporting our advocacy efforts and staying informed about ongoing policy developments.
