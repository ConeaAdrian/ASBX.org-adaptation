Why Choose ASBX.org?

Benefits of our geolocation search tool

Our powerful geolocation search tool, integrated with the Asbestos Claims Case Value Calculator, is an invaluable resource for individuals seeking information on asbestos exposure job sites and potential compensation. By providing accurate, up-to-date data based on Asbestos Trust Funds approved locations, and streamlining the compensation estimation process, we empower victims and their families to take control of their asbestos exposure history and make informed decisions about their health and compensation claims.

  • Bankruptcy site lists
  • Job and industry codes
  • Navy Ship records
  • Civil site lists
  • Product Identification for civil and bankruptcy.

Ease of Use: Simply input your search criteria, such as industry, job, and age, and our geolocation tool will provide a list of relevant job sites.

Precision: Locate Asbestos Trust Funds approved job sites in your area or nationwide with pinpoint accuracy.

Comprehensive Data: Our database contains a vast number (over 400K) of asbestos exposure job sites across various industries & jobs, ensuring that you will find all the information you need to understand your asbestos claims.
