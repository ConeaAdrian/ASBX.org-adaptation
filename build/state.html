Florida: Statute of Limitation for Filing Asbestos Claims

In the State of Florida, the time frame for filing asbestos claims, also called the statute of limitations, is as follows:

  • Four years (4) from the diagnosis date
  • Two years (2) from the time of death (wrongful death claim)
