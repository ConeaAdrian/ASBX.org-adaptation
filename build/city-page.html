Miami, FL : Add Job-Sites to Your Mesothelioma Claim

The amount of mesothelioma compensation you receive is determined by the number job sites, the types of jobs you had, and the duration of each job.

Learn how to add job-sites to your mesothelioma compensation claim calculator.

  1. Search job site by “Name” or by “First Letter”.
  2. Select job-type that you performed
  3. Select time frame of your employment
  4. Click on “Add + to your claim” button
  5. When finished adding all jobs, click on “Calculate” button

Remember to include as many jobs as you recall in your claims. If you've worked in another state, utilize the search bar at the top of the page, select your job and city, and add them to your list.
