Miami, FL: Mesothelioma Compensation Amounts

Asbestos-exposed workers in Miami, FL, diagnosed with mesothelioma can claim financial compensation. This information apply on the maximum payouts only from Asbestos Trust Funds without ratio* calculation.

Claim Type ER Claims AVG Claims IR Claims
Mesothelioma $2,098,134 $2,549,594 $6,203,194

Legend

ER Claims = Expedite Review claims. Paid in 60 days after claims are filed.

AVG Claims = Average Review claims. Paid in 90 - 120 days after claims are filed.

IR Claims = Individual Review claims. Paid in one to two years after claims are filed.

Ratio = Current payout rate established by each trust.
