    Compensation Available for Asbestos Victims In Miami, FL

    If you or a loved one has been worked in Miami, FL and later been diagnosed with an asbestos-related disease such as mesothelioma, lung cancer, or asbestosis, there are various forms of compensation available to you. The primary option include compensation from asbestos trust funds. This information apply on the maximum payouts only from Asbestos Trust Funds without ratio* calculation.

    Asbestosis Compensation Amounts

    If you have been diagnosed with severe asbestosis due to workplace exposure in Miami, FL, you may qualify for financial compensation from relevant trust funds. Below are the types of claims and their respective total available amounts.

    Claim Type ER Claims AVG Claims IR Claims
    Mesothelioma $2,098,134 $2,549,594 $6,203,194

    Legend

    ER Claims = Expedite Review claims. Paid in 60 days after claims are filed.

    AVG Claims = Average Review claims. Paid in 90 - 120 days after claims are filed.

    IR Claims = Individual Review claims. Paid in one to two years after claims are filed.

    Ratio = Current payout rate established by each trust.
