Asbestos Trust Funds

Asbestos trust funds are established by companies that have filed for bankruptcy due to asbestos-related lawsuits. These funds are used to compensate individuals who have developed illnesses related to asbestos exposure, including mesothelioma, lung cancer, and asbestosis. Asbestos trust funds are typically managed by trustees and are funded through a combination of company assets and insurance policies.
