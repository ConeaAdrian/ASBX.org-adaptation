About Us

Our Vision

The Asbestos Exposure Awareness Center (ASBX) operates with a clear and unwavering mission: to provide mesothelioma victims and their families with the information they need, to receive the compensation they deserve. Through its website, ASBX.org, ASBX equips victims and their families with a vast array of resources, covering a wide range of existing asbestos and mesothelioma-related research topics. This information empowers asbestos-exposed victims and their families to make informed decisions regarding their next steps following a mesothelioma diagnosis and prepares them to be their own advocates.

In addition to a catalog of informational resources, ASBX also provides victims and families with an Asbestos Exposure Case Value Calculator, which allows them to calculate their potential claim along with a financial compensation estimate. The calculator leverages the nation’s largest database of industries and employers responsible for asbestos exposure by allowing victims and their families to search by state, city, job site and/or company. Once a job site and/or company is identified, the Asbestos Exposure Case Value Calculator assigns victims and their families a personalized value to their claim by processing the details of their exposure and finding relevant asbestos trust funds, companies and other sources that may pay the compensation.

Values from this powerful tool can then be used to establish a dialogue between the affected party and some of the nation's top asbestos-related disease lawyers. This connection can either be facilitated by ASBX, or users may take this data to any attorney with whom they wish to represent them.

Our Mission

Decades of asbestos exposure has left tens of thousands of people and their families vulnerable to the detrimental effects of this substance. Despite an end to its usage in the 1980s, asbestos-laden materials still pose a risk to many, and the legacy of this toxic substance still lingers. Mesothelioma can take between 20 and 60 years after asbestos exposure to develop, meaning that many individuals who were exposed to asbestos several decades ago are still at risk of developing mesothelioma or other asbestos-related diseases.

ASBX believes that individuals and their families should not bear the burden of an asbestos-related disease on their own. The companies who knowingly or unknowingly exposed their employees/workers to asbestos can, and should, be held accountable for the damage asbestos has caused. Accountability is not only a matter of justice but also a fundamental step in preventing further harm.

Our Commitment

At ASBX, we recognize the urgent need for support, guidance and justice for those affected by asbestos. By providing victims and their families with a wealth of relevant knowledge and tools for calculating the value of one’s case for an asbestos-related disease, we help ensure that victims and their families are empowered to seek justice. We understand the significance of one’s journey from diagnosis to compensation, and ASBX is committed to simplifying what can be an incredibly stressful process, as well as paving a path towards a fair resolution.
